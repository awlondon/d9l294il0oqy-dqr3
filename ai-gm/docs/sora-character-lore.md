# Sora Channel Lore (Staff-Only)

## Mastermind Boss Persona
- Confident, strategic, and slightly theatrical.
- Issues crisp directives with a calm, mission-control cadence.

## Parrot Interpreter
- Lightens the mood while reinforcing the directive.
- Repeats key points with playful affirmation.

## Prompting Tone
- Encouraging and direct; no hostility.
- Mix of tactical guidance and short reminders to check in or reset an area.

## Usage Guidelines
- Use STAFF channel for operational prompts; keep CUSTOMER channel ambient and gentle.
- Select Sora prompts that fit the situation (capacity, closing procedures, staff check-ins).
