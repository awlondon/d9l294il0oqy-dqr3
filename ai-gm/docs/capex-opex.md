# CapEx and OpEx Summary

## CapEx (One-Time)
- Displays & mounts: 10 commercial displays @ ~$950 + 10 mounts @ ~$150 → **~$11,000**.
- Media players (SBCs/NUCs): 10 units @ ~$300 → **~$3,000**.
- PoE switches and injectors: rackmount switch + injectors → **~$1,500**.
- NAS and UPS: edge cache + power backup → **~$2,100** (NAS ~$1,200, UPS ~$900).
- Cabling, terminations, and installation labor: pulls, conduit, labeling → **~$7,000**.
- Rack accessories and power distribution: shelves, patch panels, PDUs → **~$620**.
- **Estimated total: ~ $25,220** (illustrative midpoint).

## Lifecycle & Replacement
- Displays: 4–5 year refresh; plan 20–25% of fleet replaced each year after Year 3.
- SBCs/NUCs: 3–4 year lifecycle depending on thermal environment.
- UPS batteries: replace every 3 years; full UPS swap at 6 years if efficiency drops.
- Networking (switches/PoE): 5-year expected life with annual firmware updates.
- Recommended annual replacement reserve: **3–5% of CapEx (~$750–$1,250/year)** to cover aging hardware and spares.

## OpEx (Annual)
- Hosting and bandwidth: cloud runtime + CDN for assets → **~$2,160/year (~$180/mo)**.
- Power consumption: incremental draw for displays/players during active hours → **~$480/year (~$40/mo)**.
- Content services (Sora/content pipeline): text-to-video prompts, QA, and upload cadence → **~$3,000/year (~$250/mo)**.
- Managed services and monitoring: patching, alerting, spares logistics → **~$2,640/year (~$220/mo)**.
- **Estimated total: ~ $8,280/year** (rounded to ~$8.2k).

## Disclaimer
Numbers are illustrative, not investment, legal, or tax advice, and are subject to change as vendor quotes and operating data evolve.
