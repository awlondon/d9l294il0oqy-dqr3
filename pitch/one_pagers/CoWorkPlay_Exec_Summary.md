# CoWorkPlay Executive Summary

Co.work.PLAY Cafe blends coworking, cafe hospitality, and supervised toddler play. The Phoenix flagship will validate the operating model and serve as the brand/training hub. The franchising program extends the concept to family-dense markets with remote/hybrid workforces.

## Highlights
- Flexible memberships and day passes with supervised play.
- Strong demand from parents needing safe, short-duration childcare and community.
- Multiple store formats to fit diverse real estate footprints.
- Franchise economics aimed at attractive payback with disciplined build-out costs.
