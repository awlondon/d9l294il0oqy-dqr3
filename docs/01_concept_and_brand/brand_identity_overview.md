# Co.work.play Café Brand Identity Overview

## Brand Essence
Co.work.play Café blends a modern coworking environment with supervised play and a welcoming café. The identity must balance credibility for working parents with warmth and delight for kids, making the space feel productive, safe, and lively.

## Personality & Tone
- **Modern & resourceful:** Confident, organized, and tech-aware to reassure working adults.
- **Playful & welcoming:** Friendly and colorful touches signal that kids are expected and celebrated.
- **Hospitality-forward:** Warmth and care in language, service, and visual cues.

## Core Audiences
- **Remote/hybrid parents** needing a reliable workspace and childcare support.
- **Caregivers and kids** looking for playful, supervised experiences.
- **Partners and franchisees** who need a clear, replicable brand system.

## Visual Pillars
- **Structured wordmark:** A text-first logo that clearly spells out the hybrid offer: coworking + play + café.
- **Color contrast:** Neutral grounding tones paired with joyful accent brights for “PLAY.”
- **Type pairing:** Technical sans-serif for work cues, rounded display for play, elegant serif/script for hospitality.

## System Components
- **Logo & lockups:** Documented in `brand_logo_spec.md` with clear color and spacing rules.
- **Color palette:** Detailed in `brand_color_palette.md`, including neutrals and the PLAY letter colors.
- **Typography:** Usage rules and hierarchy in `brand_typography.md`.
- **Applications:** Real-world guidance for signage, merch, and digital touchpoints in `brand_applications_guide.md`.

## File Organization
- Working and final logo assets belong in `/media/branding/logo_master/` (vector) and `/media/branding/logo_raster/` (raster).
- Mockups for signage, merch, and digital should live in `/media/branding/mockups/`.
- This folder (`/docs/01_concept_and_brand/`) houses all written brand standards to keep designers, marketers, and franchisees aligned.
