# Franchise Business Model

## Purpose
Outline the commercial structure, responsibilities, and unit economics for Co.work.PLAY Café franchisees and the franchisor.

## Fee Structure
- **Initial franchise fee:** $50,000 (includes site review, design kit, training, and launch support).
- **Royalty:** 6% of gross sales, paid weekly.
- **Brand fund:** 2% of gross sales for national/region-wide marketing and content creation.
- **Technology fee:** $500/month per unit for POS, app, and security stack (subject to vendor pass-through changes).
- **Design & construction services (optional):** 3–5% of hard costs if franchisor manages build-out.

## Franchisee Responsibilities
- Secure site, lease, and local permits.
- Fund build-out and working capital; implement approved design kit-of-parts.
- Hire and train staff to franchisor standards; maintain required certifications and background checks.
- Operate daily café, cowork, and play programs within policy; uphold safety ratios and SOPs.
- Participate in marketing calendar and report weekly KPIs (revenue mix, conversion, incidents, retention).

## Franchisor Responsibilities
- Maintain brand standards, menu development, and supplier agreements.
- Provide design guidelines, prototype plans, and FF&E specifications for small/medium/large formats.
- Deliver training: pre-open, on-site launch, and recurring refreshers; maintain LMS and Sora video library.
- Operate support desk for technology, safety incidents, and guest experience escalations.
- Run national brand campaigns and coordinate partnerships (employer stipends, parenting networks, educators).

## Unit Economics (Typical Medium Footprint ~6.5k SF)
- **Startup costs:** $1.4M–$1.7M (build-out, FF&E, opening inventory, pre-open payroll, working capital).
- **Revenue mix (stabilized monthly ≈ $165k):** 44% memberships/day passes, 29% café, 17% parties/events/classes, 10% meeting rooms/retail.
- **COGS:** ~19% blended (higher for café, lower for services).
- **Labor:** ~34% of revenue (host, play attendants, café team, supervisor/lead).
- **Occupancy:** ~$22/SF NNN → ~$12k/month plus utilities.
- **EBITDA range:** 22–28% once stabilized, dependent on staffing discipline and occupancy.

## Growth & Territory
- Standard territory: 3-mile radius or 100,000 people, adjusted for density and trade areas.
- Multi-unit development agreements encouraged for metro clusters; phased opening with shared management bench.
- Transfer and renewal terms follow FTC/FDD guidelines; performance clauses tied to KPI thresholds.

## Support Model by Phase
- **T-24 to T-12 weeks:** Site selection, LOI guidance, landlord pitch materials.
- **T-12 to T-4 weeks:** Construction review, equipment ordering, hiring plan, LMS access, marketing launch kit.
- **T-4 to T+4 weeks:** On-site training, soft open playbooks, post-opening KPI huddles.
- **Ongoing:** Quarterly business reviews, menu updates, technology enhancements, and peer community roundtables.
