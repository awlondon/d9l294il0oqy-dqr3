# Cleaning and Safety Checklists

## Daily
- Sanitize tables, chairs, high-touch surfaces, and play equipment.
- Sweep/mop floors; empty trash and diaper receptacles frequently.
- Restock restroom supplies and check changing stations.

## Weekly
- Deep clean play structures and soft goods; launder washable items.
- Inspect for loose hardware, damaged padding, or trip hazards; document and resolve.
- Test emergency exits, alarms, and first-aid supplies.
