# Cleaning & Safety Checklists  
Co.work.play Café

> This document defines **who does what**, **how often**, and **what counts as “done”**  
> for cleaning and safety procedures across the space.

---

## 1. Zones Covered

- Entry / Host & Retail
- Café Bar & Café Seating
- Cowork Areas (Open + Quiet + Phone Rooms)
- Main Toddler Play
- Baby / Under-2 & Sensory Room
- Multi-Use / Party Room
- Restrooms
- Back-of-House / Kitchen / Storage
- Exterior Entry (immediate frontage)

Each checklist indicates: **Responsible Role** + **Frequency**.

---

## 2. Daily Opening Checklist (Before First Guests)

### 2.1 Entry / Host & Retail  
**Responsible:** Host + Support  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening

- [ ] Sweep/vacuum entry floor.
- [ ] Wipe host stand (disinfect high-touch areas: counter, card reader, pens).
- [ ] Clean glass doors and sidelights (fingerprints, smudges).
- [ ] Ensure retail shelves are tidy, fronted, and dust-free.
- [ ] Confirm stroller parking zone is clear and marked.
- [ ] Check for tripping hazards (mats flat, cables secured).
- [ ] Confirm emergency exits and exit paths are unobstructed.

### 2.2 Café Bar & Seating  
**Responsible:** Café Team  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening

- [ ] Sanitize all bar surfaces and counters.
- [ ] Wipe café tables and chairs (tops and arms).
- [ ] Check chairs and stools for stability.
- [ ] Clean pastry case glass and interior surfaces (as allowed).
- [ ] Sweep/mop café floor as needed.
- [ ] Confirm trash cans are empty, liners replaced.
- [ ] Check espresso machine, grinders, and brewer exteriors are clean.

### 2.3 Cowork Areas (Open & Quiet)  
**Responsible:** Host or Assigned Floor Support  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening

- [ ] Wipe cowork tables and desk surfaces.
- [ ] Wipe chair arms and backs.
- [ ] Check power strips/outlets for visible damage.
- [ ] Remove abandoned items; set aside in lost & found.
- [ ] Confirm phone rooms are clean and ventilated (door open temporarily).

### 2.4 Main Toddler Play  
**Responsible:** Play Attendant  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening

- [ ] Walk the entire play structure; look for loose bolts, sharp edges, or broken pieces.
- [ ] Check matting and padding; ensure no tears or areas peeling up.
- [ ] Inspect toys for breakage; pull any broken items out of circulation.
- [ ] Wipe high-touch surfaces (rails, handles, slides) with approved disinfectant.
- [ ] Ensure all gates and barriers latch and unlatch correctly.
- [ ] Check posted rules and signage are visible and intact.

### 2.5 Baby / Under-2 & Sensory Room  
**Responsible:** Play Attendant  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening

- [ ] Inspect soft mats and cushions; ensure they are clean and properly placed.
- [ ] Wipe sensory toys and equipment per cleaning schedule.
- [ ] Confirm rockers/gliders are stable and in good condition.
- [ ] Remove any small-choking-hazard items inappropriate for under-2s.

### 2.6 Multi-Use / Party Room  
**Responsible:** Host or Event Lead  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening

- [ ] Reset room to default configuration (tables/chairs).
- [ ] Wipe tables and chairs.
- [ ] Sweep/mop as needed.
- [ ] Check AV equipment and cords are safely placed or stored.

### 2.7 Restrooms  
**Responsible:** Assigned Cleaner / Support  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening (and throughout the day)

- [ ] Clean and disinfect toilets and urinals.
- [ ] Wipe sinks, faucets, and counters.
- [ ] Refill soap, paper towels, toilet paper.
- [ ] Empty trash and replace liners.
- [ ] Mop floor as needed; check for leaks or standing water.
- [ ] Confirm baby changing stations are clean and stocked.

### 2.8 Back-of-House / Kitchen / Storage  
**Responsible:** Café Team + Manager  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening

- [ ] Sanitize food prep surfaces.
- [ ] Check refrigerator and freezer temps; log as required.
- [ ] Ensure chemicals are stored properly and labeled.
- [ ] Confirm storage shelves are organized and off the floor as required by health code.

### 2.9 Exterior Entry (Immediate Area)  
**Responsible:** Host or Assigned Support  
**Frequency:** Daily, before opening

- [ ] Check for trash, debris, and spills.
- [ ] Sweep immediate entry threshold.
- [ ] Confirm external signage and sandwich boards are clean and properly placed (if used).

## 3. During-the-Day Cleaning Checklist

### 3.1 Hourly or As-Needed Touch-Ups  
**Responsible:** All roles, led by Manager

- [ ] Wipe café tables between guest turnovers.
- [ ] Spot-clean spills immediately (use wet floor signs if needed).
- [ ] Empty trash if more than ~75% full.
- [ ] Quick restroom check every 60–90 minutes:
  - [ ] No major messes, supplies stocked.

### 3.2 Play Area Midday Reset  
**Responsible:** Play Attendant  
**Frequency:** At least once mid-shift; more during peak

- [ ] Return toys to designated zones (blocks, pretend kitchen, books).
- [ ] Quickly wipe heavily used items (per “quick clean” protocol).
- [ ] Remove any toys mouthed excessively or contaminated (set aside for deep clean).
- [ ] Re-check for hazards (broken toys, loose pieces, wet spots on floor).

---

## 4. Closing Checklist (End of Day)

### 4.1 Entry & Host  
- [ ] Remove trash; replace liners.
- [ ] Wipe host stand, door handles, and high-touch surfaces.
- [ ] Tidy retail shelves; consolidate products.
- [ ] Turn off any entry displays, lights per policy.

### 4.2 Café Bar & Seating  
- [ ] Fully clean espresso machine per procedure.
- [ ] Wipe all bar surfaces and equipment exteriors.
- [ ] Clean and sanitize café tables and chairs.
- [ ] Sweep and mop café floor.
- [ ] Discard or store food items according to food safety rules.
- [ ] Remove trash and recycling; clean bins as needed.

### 4.3 Cowork Areas  
- [ ] Wipe all tables and desks.
- [ ] Wipe chair arms and backs.
- [ ] Organize chairs and power cords.
- [ ] Light vacuum or sweep.

### 4.4 Main Toddler Play & Baby Room  
- [ ] Collect and sort toys; separate “needs deep clean” items.
- [ ] Wipe high-touch surfaces (rails, handles, shared toys) with disinfectant.
- [ ] Spot mop any soiled areas on mats or floor.
- [ ] Confirm gates are secured for the night.

### 4.5 Multi-Use / Party Room  
- [ ] Clear tables and surfaces.
- [ ] Wipe tables and chairs.
- [ ] Sweep/mop floor.
- [ ] Reset to default or next-day configuration.

### 4.6 Restrooms  
- [ ] Full clean (toilets, sinks, counters, mirrors).
- [ ] Restock supplies.
- [ ] Mop floors.
- [ ] Empty all trash.

### 4.7 BOH / Kitchen / Storage  
- [ ] Clean prep surfaces and sinks.
- [ ] Sweep and mop floors.
- [ ] Secure food storage areas.
- [ ] Store cleaning chemicals safely.

---

## 5. Weekly / Deep Cleaning Tasks

**Responsible:** Manager to schedule; may be external cleaning vendor or staff.

- [ ] Deep clean play equipment (per manufacturer guidelines).  
- [ ] Launder or deep clean soft play items and cushions.  
- [ ] Dust vents, high surfaces, and light fixtures.  
- [ ] Move furniture and clean underneath in café and cowork.  
- [ ] Deep descale espresso machine and coffee equipment (if not done more frequently).  
- [ ] Inspect walls and corners for scuffs; touch up as needed.  

---

## 6. Safety Checks (Non-Cleaning)

### 6.1 Weekly Safety Checklist

- [ ] Check all emergency exits and hardware.  
- [ ] Test emergency lighting (if applicable).  
- [ ] Confirm fire extinguishers are in place and inspected (check tags).  
- [ ] Inspect play structure fasteners and joints.  
- [ ] Check for trip hazards (frayed rugs, loose thresholds, loose cables).  
- [ ] Review incident log for patterns and possible mitigations.

### 6.2 Monthly Manager Review

- [ ] Review cleaning logs for completeness.  
- [ ] Spot-audit actual cleanliness vs checklists.  
- [ ] Identify recurring issues and adjust staffing or process.  

---

## 7. Documentation & Logs

- Maintain **daily cleaning checklist sheets** (paper or digital) with:
  - Date, time, initials.
- Maintain **incident and safety log** for:
  - Injuries, near-misses, and notable safety issues.
- Store logs per policy for required retention period.

This document is a template and should be customized to local health and safety codes.
