# Staffing and Shift Structure

- **Roles**: Host, Play Attendant, Barista/Café, Manager/Lead.
- **Weekday mornings**: Heavier host/play coverage for young families; lean café team.
- **Afternoons**: Balance café and play as school-age siblings arrive; add second attendant on busy days.
- **Weekends/parties**: Dedicated attendant for party room plus café support; manager on duty for events.
- **Training**: Cross-train hosts and café team on basic play coverage for breaks; maintain CPR/first-aid certifications.
