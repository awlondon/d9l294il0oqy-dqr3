# Manager SOP

- Scheduling and staffing: balance host, play attendant, café, and manager coverage by daypart.
- Ordering and inventory: maintain par levels for café and cleaning supplies.
- Reporting: daily sales, membership check-ins, incidents, and KPI rollups.
- Coaching: run pre-shift huddles, service observations, and weekly 1:1s.
- Franchise communication: submit required reports and maintain brand standards.
