# Manager SOP  
Co.work.play Café

## 1. Role Purpose

The Manager is responsible for:

- Overall **store performance** (experience, safety, and P&L).
- Leading and developing the Host, Play Attendant, and Café team.
- Ensuring adherence to brand standards and franchise requirements (where applicable).
- Acting as the primary liaison to the franchisor / HQ.

---

## 2. Daily Rhythm

### 2.1 Opening Responsibilities

- Confirm staffing: all critical roles present or covered.  
- Quick huddle with team:
  - Review reservations and parties.  
  - Identify any special needs or constraints.  
  - Set focus for the day (e.g., membership conversions, café attach rate, safety reminders).

- Walk the space:
  - Entry, play areas, café, cowork, restrooms, BOH.  
  - Check for cleanliness, safety issues, and signage.

- Ensure POS, radios, and other systems are online.

### 2.2 Midday Responsibilities

- Monitor flow and guest experience:
  - Step in to help at Host, Play, or Café when needed.  
  - Check in with guests and members (walk the floor).  

- Manage breaks:
  - Ensure coverage for all key roles during breaks and lunches.

- Handle escalations:
  - Complaints about service, safety, or policies.  
  - Conflicts between guests.

### 2.3 Closing Responsibilities

- Confirm that all children and adults are checked out.  
- Ensure end-of-day cleaning tasks are completed.  
- Verify cash handling and daily sales reports.  
- Lock and secure the premises per policy.

---

## 3. People Management

- **Hiring & onboarding** (in conjunction with HQ, if franchised).  
- **Scheduling**:
  - Match staffing levels to forecasted demand (school days vs weekends, party bookings).  
- **Coaching**:
  - Review performance, correct behavior gently in real time.  
  - Conduct regular 1:1s with key staff.

- **Training**:
  - Ensure all new hires complete required video modules and SOP reviews.  
  - Schedule refreshers and safety drills.

---

## 4. Guest Recovery & Escalations

When a guest is upset:

1. Listen fully; avoid interrupting.  
2. Empathize: “I can see this has been frustrating.”  
3. Clarify facts and restate what you can do.  
4. Offer appropriate remedies within policy:
   - Re-made drink, discounted or comped item.  
   - Future credit or free play pass (for larger issues).  

Log significant complaints and resolutions so HQ can see patterns.

---

## 5. Safety, Compliance & Incident Handling

- Own the **incident log** and ensure entries are complete.  
- Coordinate any **911 / EMS calls** when needed.  
- Follow reporting requirements for:
  - Injuries or medical emergencies.  
  - Property damage.  
  - Allegations of misconduct.

- Ensure compliance with:
  - Health department inspections and requirements.  
  - Fire and building codes (exits clear; capacity limits respected).

---

## 6. Financial & Reporting Responsibilities

- Review daily/weekly sales and labor costs.  
- Track key KPIs:
  - Membership count and churn.  
  - Day-pass volume.  
  - Café attach rate (avg spend per adult).  
  - Party/event bookings.

- Report metrics to franchisor / HQ using the agreed template and cadence.

---

## 7. Relationship with Franchisor / HQ (For Franchised Units)

- Participate in regular check-ins (calls, video, site visits).  
- Implement system-wide updates:
  - Menu changes.  
  - Policy changes.  
  - Training updates.

- Provide feedback back up the chain (what’s working, what’s not).

This SOP is supported by Manager training modules focusing on leadership, financial basics, and brand standards.
