# Flagship P&L Model Specification

Structured so the Excel/Sheets file can be refreshed as new inputs arrive and mirrored into franchise models.

## Inputs
- **Physical plant:** Square footage by zone (cowork, play, café, back-of-house), rentable SF, ceiling height notes.
- **Occupancy:** Base rent per SF, CAM/NNN charges, escalation schedule, utilities estimates, insurance.
- **Labor:** Staffing plan by daypart (hosts, play attendants, café, party captains, manager/shift lead), hourly rates, benefits/taxes, training hours, overtime and relief assumptions.
- **Pricing:** Membership tiers, day pass pricing, party packages, café menu pricing, meeting room rates, class/camp price points, discounts/promos.
- **Volume drivers:** Active member counts by tier, attendance/visit frequency, day pass volume, café attach rate, party bookings, meeting room utilization, program enrollments, corporate/HR bundles.
- **CapEx & financing:** Build-out budget, pre-opening spend, working capital, interest rate/term, draw schedule.

## Revenue Streams
- **Memberships:** MRR by tier, prepaid conversions, corporate bundles, churn/retention curves.
- **Passes & time-based use:** Day passes, add-on hours, guest fees.
- **Café & retail:** Beverage/food sales, grab-and-go, merch; attach rates by visit type.
- **Events:** Parties, private rentals, camps/classes, meeting rooms.
- **Other:** Sponsorships/brand partnerships, lockers, printing, ancillary services.

## Major Cost Buckets
- **Labor:** Wages, taxes/benefits, training, relief staffing, management bonus accruals.
- **COGS:** Café/retail food & bev, paper goods, packaging, program materials.
- **Occupancy:** Base rent, CAM/NNN, utilities, janitorial, security, waste.
- **Marketing:** Local spend, grand-opening ramp, ongoing brand fund contributions.
- **Technology:** POS, Wi-Fi/network, security systems, AI-GM platform, SaaS stack.
- **Operations & maintenance:** Supplies, maintenance contracts, CapEx reserve, insurance.

## Outputs
- **Unit economics:** Revenue mix, gross margin, labor-to-revenue %, occupancy cost %, contribution margin.
- **EBITDA & cash:** Monthly EBITDA, cash burn to breakeven, free cash flow after debt service.
- **Capital metrics:** Build-out cost per SF, per-seat cost, per-member acquisition cost, payback period (months) and IRR.
- **Sensitivity scenarios:** Base/conservative/upside toggles on rent, wage inflation, member ramp, café attach, party volume, CapEx overage.
- **Dashboard:** KPI summary with charts for membership growth, utilization, and cash runway.
