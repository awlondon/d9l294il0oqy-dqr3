# Franchisor P&L and 10-Year Projection

Built on the 100-store rollout cadence with scenario toggles to stress-test royalty durability and platform margins.

## Inputs & Assumptions
- **Rollout schedule:** Store openings by quarter/year per `docs/franchise/100_store_rollout_plan.md`, with delays and early-open toggles.
- **Franchise fees:** Per-unit fee, recognition timing (signing vs. opening), renewal fees.
- **Royalty structure:** % of gross sales, minimums, timing of ramp from soft-open to stabilization.
- **Brand fund/marketing contributions:** % of sales, remitted monthly; usage assumptions for national campaigns.
- **Tech/platform fees:** Per-unit or per-member recurring fees for AI-GM, POS integrations, training LMS.
- **Training & launch services:** Charges for pre-opening training, onsite launch support, and travel pass-throughs.
- **Corporate overhead:** HQ staffing plan, G&A, legal, insurance, R&D/engineering, community support team.
- **Capital & cash:** Initial raise, debt facilities, interest rate/terms, support for working-capital advances to franchisees.

## Outputs
- **Revenue by stream and year:** Franchise fees, royalties, marketing fund contributions, tech/platform fees, training/onboarding services.
- **Gross margin:** Cost to deliver tech/support/training vs. fees collected.
- **Operating expenses:** Headcount ramp, legal/compliance, product/engineering, marketing, community success.
- **EBITDA and cash runway:** Annual EBITDA, cumulative cash, and burn rate relative to capital raised.
- **Scenario views:** Base, conservative (slower openings and lower AUV), upside (faster cadence, higher AUV, earlier tech adoption).
- **Unit count & AUV:** Cumulative open units, AUV by cohort, churn/closures sensitivity.
- **Board pack summary:** Charts for revenue mix, EBITDA margin trend, and cash runway by quarter for the first 12 quarters.
