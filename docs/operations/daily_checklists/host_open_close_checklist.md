# Host Open/Close Checklist

## Open
- Turn on lobby lights, music level, and display screens.
- Verify wristband/QR printer readiness; stock wristbands and stickers.
- Log into POS/membership system; confirm cash drawer starting balance (if used).
- Walk entry/lobby for hazards; confirm stroller/parking zones marked.
- Review birthday/party schedule and expected tours; prep welcome notes.

## Mid-Shift
- Greet each party; verify authorized adults and apply matching wristbands/QRs.
- Confirm wellness attestation reminders; redirect visibly ill guests per policy.
- Monitor lobby cleanliness; restock forms, wipes, and tissues.
- Track capacity by zone with play attendants; meter entry when needed.

## Close
- Reconcile wristbands issued vs. check-outs; confirm all children matched to adults.
- Close cash drawer and POS sessions; print end-of-day report.
- Tidy lobby, wipe high-touch surfaces, restock for morning.
- Log notable incidents/feedback for manager review.
