# Play Area Open/Close and Cleaning Checklist

## Open
- Inspect play structures and mats for damage/hazards; remove and tag any unsafe items.
- Check under-2 zone mats and toys; verify age-appropriate items only.
- Stock wipes/sanitizer at stations; set up “dirty toy” bins with liners.
- Confirm noise/occupancy monitors are powered and visible to staff.

## During Service
- Rotate attendants through zones every 60–90 minutes to stay fresh and observant.
- Sweep for tripping hazards, spills, and misplaced café items; redirect food to café.
- Move soiled toys to “dirty toy” bin; spray/wipe high-touch surfaces hourly.
- Log incidents (bumps/falls, conflicts) with time, child initials, and action taken.
- Meter entry if capacity limits approached; coordinate with host.

## Close
- Collect all “dirty toy” bins; follow the toy hygiene SOP for sanitizing and drying.
- Wipe down mats, slides, railings, and handles with approved disinfectant.
- Vacuum/sweep play zones; check for lost items and tag for lost-and-found.
- Power down monitors as required; secure any movable play pieces.
- Record cleaning completion in the daily log; note any maintenance issues.
