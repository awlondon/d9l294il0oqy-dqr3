# Café Open/Close and Food Safety Checklist

## Open
- Calibrate espresso/coffee equipment; run test shots and grind checks.
- Check fridge/freezer temps; log readings; verify food within date codes.
- Set up handwashing, glove, and sanitizer stations; restock towels and labels.
- Prep pastries/grab-and-go; set allergen signage and ingredient books.
- Confirm POS menu and printer status; test mobile order routing.

## During Service
- Maintain handwashing and glove changes per task; sanitize high-touch surfaces hourly.
- Time-stamp and rotate prepared items; discard per holding-time policy.
- Track café inventory for restocks; log waste/spills.
- Coordinate with play attendants and party captain on orders and deliveries.

## Close
- Count and log leftover items/waste; secure perishables and label/date.
- Deep clean equipment (wands, baskets, grinders, pitchers); run detergent cycles where required.
- Wipe and sanitize counters, handles, and POS devices; sweep/mop café and behind-counter areas.
- Empty trash/compost/recycling; break down boxes; restock for morning.
- Record closing temps and cleaning completion in food safety log.
