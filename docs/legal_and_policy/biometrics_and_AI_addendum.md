# Biometrics and AI Addendum

Supplement to the Privacy Policy and Membership Terms; for adult users only.

## Biometrics Scope
- **Optional enrollment:** Adult members may opt-in to biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprint or palm) for check-in and cashless payments.
- **Purpose:** Convenience for login/payment and faster account lookup; not used for surveillance or access denial beyond authentication.
- **Children:** No biometric collection or matching for children.
- **Consent:** Explicit written consent required; alternative methods (QR, PIN, card) always available.

## Data Handling
- Templates stored as encrypted hashes via third-party biometric provider; no raw images retained.
- Biometric templates deleted upon withdrawal of consent or account closure (subject to legal retention rules).
- Access limited to need-to-know staff; audit logs maintained for biometric events where supported.

## AI-GM Boundaries
- **Inputs:** Cameras, occupancy/noise sensors, POS and membership data, manual incident reports.
- **Uses:** Occupancy monitoring, crowding/noise alerts, environmental anomaly detection, cleaning cues.
- **Prohibited uses:** No scoring of individuals, no facial recognition of children, no automated disciplinary actions.
- **Notifications:** Outputs go to staff tablets/monitors for human review and action.

## Notices
- Post signage at entrances describing camera/sensor use and optional biometrics for adults.
- Publish this addendum alongside the Privacy Policy and Terms; provide downloadable copies on the website and app.
