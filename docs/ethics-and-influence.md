# Ethics and Influence Guardrails

## What We Will Not Do
- No high-pressure sales during events or tours.
- No levels, ranks, or downlinesâ€”ever.
- No pseudo-therapy, forced vulnerability circles, or manipulative tactics.
- No surprise upsells or hidden commitments.

## What We Will Do
- Transparent pricing, policies, and response windows for membership offers.
- Optional, single-level referral thank-yous with light perks only.
- Community events that are value-first, family-safe, and clearly labeled.
- Clear consent for communications; easy opt-outs across email, SMS, and app notifications.
- Documented scripts that emphasize autonomy and informed choice.

## How to Use This Doc
- Link to this page from any feature touching referrals, events, or contributor programs.
- Include a short reminder in code comments: keep incentives flat and transparent.
- Treat this as a non-negotiable standard for franchisees, staff, and partners.
