# Fee and Royalty Structure

- **Initial franchise fee**: Target range $75k–$100k depending on market size and support level.
- **Ongoing royalty**: Percentage of gross revenue (e.g., 6–7%).
- **Marketing fund**: National/brand fund contribution (e.g., 2% of revenue) for shared campaigns.
- **Optional services**: Design/project management support, training extensions, and pre-opening assistance.
