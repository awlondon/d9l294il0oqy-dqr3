# Fee and Royalty Structure

- **Initial franchise fee**: Target range $75k–$100k depending on market size and support level.
- **Ongoing royalty**: Percentage of gross revenue (e.g., 6–7%).
  - Paid weekly via ACH sweep from POS deposits with monthly reconciliation.
  - Royalty holidays or step-downs (e.g., 3% for first 3 months) are reserved for strategic pilots and require executive approval.
- **Marketing fund**: National/brand fund contribution (e.g., 2% of revenue) for shared campaigns.
  - Separate local store marketing spend target: ~3% of revenue retained by franchisee for grassroots activation.
  - Quarterly brand fund reporting provided to franchisees with line-of-sight to media and content investments.
- **Technology/Systems fee**: Per-location fee to cover POS, scheduling, access control, LMS, and CCTV storage; calibrated to vendor contracts.
- **Training fee**: Included in initial franchise fee for first two attendees; nominal per-person charge for additional team members or refresher sessions.
- **Design/construction support**: Optional project-management or prototype adaptation services billed hourly or as a flat package; procurement fees for FF&E pass-through when applicable.
