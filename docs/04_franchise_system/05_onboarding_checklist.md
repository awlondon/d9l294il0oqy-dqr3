# Onboarding Checklist

1. Inquiry and qualification call.
2. Territory review and preliminary approval.
3. Franchise agreement and fee payment.
4. Site selection with franchisor review and LOI/lease execution.
5. Design package kickoff; equipment and play vendor coordination.
6. Staffing plan, training enrollment, and pre-opening marketing.
7. Final inspections, soft opening, and grand opening support.
