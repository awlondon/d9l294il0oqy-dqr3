# Onboarding Checklist

1. Inquiry and qualification call (financial review, operational fit, territory goals).
2. Territory review and preliminary approval (demographic and competition scan).
3. Franchise agreement and fee payment; onboarding to LMS, brand portal, and vendor list.
4. Site selection with franchisor review and LOI/lease execution; schedule landlord work letter and test-fit.
5. Design package kickoff; equipment and play vendor coordination; establish permitting timeline and GC bid list.
6. Technology stack setup (POS, scheduling, access control, CCTV, Wi-Fi) initiated alongside design milestones.
7. Staffing plan, training enrollment, and pre-opening marketing (founding memberships, party pre-sales, influencer outreach).
8. Dry runs and compliance checks: health/fire inspections, play safety certification, and IT/PCI validation.
9. Soft opening with limited hours; HQ on-site/virtual support for first week.
10. Grand opening marketing push and first 90-day KPI cadence launched.
