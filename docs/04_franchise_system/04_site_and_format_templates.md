# Site and Format Templates

## Neighborhood Co.work.play (~5–6k sf)
- Emphasis on efficient play hall and compact café; strong parking.

## Urban Compact (~4–5k sf)
- Dense seating, multi-use rooms that flex between party and cowork functions; premium visibility and transit access.

## Flagship / Destination (~8–9k sf)
- Full program with larger party/event space, expanded café, and more generous circulation.
