# Community Board Guidelines

## Purpose
Keep the digital and physical boards safe, local, and family-friendly.

## Allowed
- Local offerings, services, and events that are family-safe.
- Requests for help, swaps, or meetups with clear contact info.
- Announcements from trusted partners that align with cafe values.

## Not allowed
- Recruiting for MLMs, high-pressure groups, or predatory schemes.
- Politics, hate speech, or anything undermining safety and belonging.
- Anonymous posts without consent to share contact details.

## Moderation standards
- All posts require admin approval before going live.
- Posts are listed chronologically by category; no algorithmic boosts.
- Admins may edit for clarity/length with author consent; otherwise reject.
- Display a QR code at the physical board linking to the digital submission form.
