# 90-Day Action Plan – Phoenix Flagship

This plan adapts an AI-generated 90-day launch framework to our actual timeline and current status (design work with BRR, early 2026 target opening).

## Phase 1: Design & Site (Days 1–30)

- [ ] Finalize Program & Design Brief with BRR.
- [ ] Shortlist and validate candidate sites (8,000–9,000 sq ft) in target Phoenix submarkets.
- [ ] Align preliminary budget (CapEx ≈ $2.2M) with Savannah / Michael / investors.
- [ ] Begin legal review on licensing and supervised play positioning (non-daycare).
- [ ] Advance digital product specs for:
      - Host check-in and membership,
      - Seat-based ordering,
      - Future AI-GM integration.

## Phase 2: Site Control & Schematic Design (Days 31–60)

- [ ] Negotiate LOI/lease on selected site.
- [ ] Complete schematic design and test fit with BRR.
- [ ] Engage play equipment vendor for detailed layout.
- [ ] Obtain initial landlord and jurisdictional feedback on TI and occupancy.
- [ ] Begin hiring pipeline for GM and key opening roles.

## Phase 3: Pre-Build Launch Prep (Days 61–90)

- [ ] Lock in core vendors (coffee, F&B, play equipment, POS/tech).
- [ ] Finalize membership pricing and day-pass structure (founding member offers).
- [ ] Build “Founding Member” waitlist campaign (web + social).
- [ ] Outline detailed training content and Sora video prompts for:
      - Host,
      - Play Attendant,
      - Café Operator,
      - Manager roles.
- [ ] Prepare construction schedule and cash flow projections for buildout and pre-opening costs.
