## Member-Facing Layer

- Web/app for:
  - Membership management,
  - Session booking (optional),
  - Seat-based ordering from assigned workstation,
  - Viewing invoices and session history.
- Seat QR / login for:
  - Ordering,
  - Light notifications (order status, policy reminders, Sora clips).

## Operations Layer

- Host check-in system with:
  - Member verification,
  - Child/guardian linkage,
  - Shared caregiver support.
- POS and Kitchen Display System (KDS) integrated with seat codes.
- Staff dashboards for:
  - Live capacity (kids, adults),
  - Open orders,
  - Cleaning/sanitization tasks.

## Back-Office & Franchise Layer

- CRM for member communications and retention.
- Analytics dashboard for:
  - Daily revenue,
  - Seat utilization,
  - Membership churn.
- Future franchise portal:
  - Design standards,
  - SOPs,
  - Training content (Sora videos),
  - Vendor links.

## AI-GM & Automation Roadmap

- Phase 1: Early AI-GM as monitoring and notification layer (capacity, noise thresholds, policy compliance).
- Phase 2: Biometric/PIN-enabled workstation login and ordering (optional, opt-in).
- Phase 3: Integration with robotic food runners when technology and reliability are sufficient, keeping the human service model intact as default.
