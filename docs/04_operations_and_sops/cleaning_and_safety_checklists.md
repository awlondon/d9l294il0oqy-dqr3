# Cleaning and Safety Checklists

## Purpose
Standardized opening, midday, and closing checklists for all zones to keep the cafe, cowork, and play areas clean, safe, and guest-ready.

## Roles
- **Host:** Entry experience, wristbands/tags, lobby cleanliness.
- **Play Attendant:** Play and under-2 zones, safety checks, toy rotation.
- **Cafe Operator:** Bar, prep, cafe seating, and FOH cleanliness.
- **Manager/Shift Lead:** Verifies completion, handles incidents, logs exceptions.

## Opening Checklist (Before Doors Open)
- Entry/Host
  - Turn on signage, music at set levels; unlock entry and test access control.
  - Wipe host stand, check wristband/tag inventory, power tablets/label printer.
  - Inspect stroller parking and retail shelves; restock sanitizer and masks.
- Cafe
  - Calibrate espresso grinders; pull test shots and log ratios.
  - Set up drip/PUC, restock milk/alt-milk, pastries, kids’ bentos, and grab-and-go.
  - Sanitize bar surfaces, handles, and condiment station; place clean rags and sanitizer buckets.
- Cowork
  - Wipe tables, check outlets/power strips, restock water carafes and napkins.
  - Clean phone booths; check ventilation and door latches.
- Play (2–5)
  - Inspect play structure for loose hardware, splinters, or damage; document findings.
  - Sanitize high-touch toys, mats, and rails; restock wipes and tissues.
  - Verify headcount signage and safety rules are visible; confirm gate latch and door alarms function.
- Under-2 Room
  - Sanitize mats, soft blocks, and seating; check rocker stability.
  - Set lighting to calming level; ensure baby-safe cleaning supplies are stored/locked.
- Party/Flex Room
  - Sweep/mop floors, set base layout, check AV and lighting.
- Restrooms
  - Stock paper, soap, diapers/wipes backup; sanitize fixtures and changing tables.
- BOH
  - Check dish machine and 3-comp sink setup; restock chemicals; test hot water.
- Safety & Logs
  - Confirm first-aid kit and incident forms; test CCTV and alarm panels.
  - Manager signs opening log in ops system.

## Midday Reset (Every 2–3 Hours)
- Entry/Host: Tidy stroller area, disinfect host stand, reset retail fronting.
- Cafe: Wipe bar and condiment, empty trash, temp-check TCS items, replenish pastries/grab-and-go.
- Cowork: Spot clean tables, sanitize phone booths/headsets, restock water carafes.
- Play: Quick sweep/vacuum, sanitize high-touch toys, reset loose items to bins, re-verify gate latch.
- Under-2: Swap out any mouthed toys to sanitation bin; wipe mats; refresh burp cloths.
- Restrooms: Wipe handles/faucets, restock paper/soap, empty diaper trash if 50% full.
- Safety: Headcounts every 30 minutes logged by play attendant; note any incident or near-miss.

## Closing Checklist
- Entry/Host: Collect lost-and-found, wipe host stand/screens, secure cash tips, lock doors/signage.
- Cafe: Deep clean espresso heads/wands, empty/clean drip tray and grinders, label/cover milk, close POS batch, wipe refrigeration gaskets, sweep/mop.
- Cowork: Sanitize tables/chairs, power down monitors/AV, collect chargers and lost items.
- Play: Sanitize mats, structure touchpoints, and high-use toys; launder cloth items; lock storage; final headcount and sweep.
- Under-2: Sanitize all soft goods; launder linens; lock cabinets with cleaning supplies.
- Party/Flex: Return to base layout; wipe surfaces; lock storage.
- Restrooms: Full clean (toilets, sinks, floors); empty trash/diaper bins; restock.
- BOH: Run dish machine final cycle; wipe sinks; store chemicals; verify laundry complete.
- Safety & Logs: Confirm alarms set; lock all gates/doors; manager signs closing checklist and incident log.

## Weekly Deep Clean Highlights
- Descale espresso machines; clean ice machine; replace water filters per schedule.
- Dust high surfaces and vents; wipe light fixtures.
- Inspect play structure hardware and foam integrity; document wear.
- Inventory and replace PPE, first-aid, and cleaning supplies.

## Documentation
- Keep completed checklists in the ops system with timestamp and initials.
- Exceptions or equipment issues are logged and escalated to manager/maintenance vendor within 24 hours.
