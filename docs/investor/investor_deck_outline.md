# Investor Deck Outline

## Founding Timeline
- **Nov 10, 2025 – Concept spark:** Video call between co-founders Alex London and Savannah Strasner + live AI prompting session creates the first “Phoenix flagship” cowork + play vision and a flagship-plus-franchise model.
- **Nov–Dec 2025 – Concept validation:** Early renderings, preliminary financials, and market sizing for remote/hybrid caregivers with toddlers.
- **2026 – Phoenix Flagship:** Targeted opening of first flagship in Phoenix metro as training hub and proof-of-concept.

## Problem & Audience
- Remote and hybrid caregivers need a place to work without being far from their toddlers.
- Employers lack family-friendly third spaces to support flexible work.

## Solution
- Cowork café with supervised toddler play space and safety-first staffing.
- Repeatable flagship-plus-franchise model.

## Market Opportunity
- Growing remote/hybrid workforce with young children.
- Lack of trusted, flexible childcare-plus-work solutions.

## Business Model
- Memberships, day passes, meeting room bookings, café revenue, parties/events.
- Franchising for local operators + corporate-owned flagships as training hubs.

## Traction & Next Steps
- Concept renderings and operational playbooks in development.
- Site selection underway for Phoenix flagship.
- Franchise recruiting and owner readiness programming planned post-flagship launch.

## Long-Term Vision / Future Growth Options

- **Airport / Transit Hubs (Future Option)**
  - Potential Phase 3–4 “Co.work.PLAY Express” concept for international airports and major transit hubs.
  - High-visibility brand presence and premium short-stay offering for traveling families.
  - To be explored only after the core flagship + neighborhood model is proven across multiple metros.
