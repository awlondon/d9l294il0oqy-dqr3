# Franchisee Profile

Qualities of ideal owner/operators for co.work.play.

## Background
- Multi-unit experience in F&B, family entertainment, childcare-adjacent concepts, or cowork.
- Comfortable with hospitality, health/safety, and service standards.
- Familiarity with local permitting/build-out processes.

## Operator Skills & Mindset
- Strong people leader; comfortable recruiting, training, and scheduling hourly teams.
- Excellent with families and children; can model a calm, safety-first culture.
- Systems-driven: embraces required tech stack (POS, membership, AI-GM, LMS) and data reporting.
- Community builder: able to partner with schools, employers, and parent groups to drive memberships.
- Hands-on during first units; willing to hire experienced GM once stabilized.
