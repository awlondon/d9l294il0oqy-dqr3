# Franchise Offering Outline

Bullet summary to hand off to counsel for the formal FDD/FA.

## What the Franchisee Receives
- **Brand & trademarks:** Rights to operate under Co.work.PLAY Cafe with approved signage and marketing assets.
- **Prototype design package:** Floor plan, finishes, furniture/fixture schedules, and approved vendor list.
- **AI-GM platform:** Access to the core platform for occupancy monitoring, incident logging, and staff alerts.
- **Training & playbook:** Pre-opening training, SOP library, and launch support toolkit.
- **Launch support:** Opening readiness checklist, marketing templates, and onsite support for soft/opening weeks.

## What the Franchisee Must Do
- **Build & fit-out:** Follow prototype design standards, safety codes, and approved vendor list; submit deviations for approval.
- **Operating standards:** Maintain required hours, staffing ratios, cleanliness, and safety protocols; uphold membership and guest policies.
- **Systems:** Use required POS, membership, AI-GM, security, and accounting systems; maintain data-sharing requirements.
- **Membership model:** Offer required membership tiers, pricing guardrails, and promotions; honor brand-wide policies.
- **Reporting:** Submit weekly sales, labor, and incident reports; participate in QA audits.

## Fee Structure
- **Franchise fee:** Paid at signing; covers territory grant and onboarding.
- **Royalty:** % of gross sales (minimums apply) remitted weekly.
- **Marketing fund:** % of gross sales for brand/national marketing.
- **Technology/platform fee:** Fixed monthly fee per unit for AI-GM, POS integrations, and LMS access.
- **Training:** Pre-opening training included; additional sessions and travel billed at cost.
