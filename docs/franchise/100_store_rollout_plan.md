# 10-Year Franchise Rollout – 100 Stores in 14 Metros

## Assumptions
- Mature store annual revenue (base case): $2.5M per store
- Average effective revenue across first few years: $2.0M per store-year (to reflect ramp-up)
- Royalty: 6% of gross sales
- Brand / marketing fund: 2% of gross sales
- Tech/platform fee: $6,000 per store per year
- Initial franchise fee: $60,000 per store
- Training/launch fee: $10,000 per store
