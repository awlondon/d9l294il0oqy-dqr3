# Opening Timeline (Target)

1. **Funding & lease**: Secure capital commitments and sign LOI/lease.
2. **Design**: Finalize layout, interior palette, and play equipment selections.
3. **Permitting**: Submit plans and respond to local authority comments.
4. **Build-out**: Construction and equipment installation; order long-lead items early.
5. **Training & soft opening**: Run staff training, pilot SOPs, and soft open for members.
6. **Grand opening**: Target early 2026 following a staged marketing campaign.
