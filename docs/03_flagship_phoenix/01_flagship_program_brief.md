# Flagship Program Brief

- **Target size**: 5,000–8,500 sf, optimized around a central play hall with adjacent café and cowork seating.
- **Program areas**: Café/bar, cowork seating, main toddler play, baby/under-2 room, party/multi-use room, back-of-house, stroller parking, and restrooms.
- **Capacity targets**: Balance ~60–80 children with matching adult seating; cafe/cowork mix flexes by time of day.
- **Design ethos**: Warm, family-forward interiors with clear sightlines for safety and supervision.
