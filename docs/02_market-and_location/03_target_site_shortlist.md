# Target Site Shortlist

- **Chandler**: Strong family density and incomes; good parking and newer retail centers. Rent assumptions: mid-$30s/sf NNN; rating 9/10.
- **Arcadia / Indian School Corridor**: Highly visible and central; mix of young families and professionals. Parking can be tighter; rating 8/10.
- **Gilbert**: Fast-growing family market with ample parking; slightly longer drive times from central Phoenix; rating 8/10.
- **Tempe / South Scottsdale**: Proximity to employers and multi-family housing; watch for parking ratios and tenant mix; rating 7/10.
