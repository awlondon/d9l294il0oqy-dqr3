# Site Selection Criteria

- **Size**: 5,000–8,500 sf with efficient rectangular footprints.
- **Parking and access**: Abundant parking, easy stroller access, and safe pedestrian routes.
- **Visibility**: High-visibility signage from main arterials; co-tenancy with family-friendly brands.
- **Neighborhood fit**: Family-dense trade areas with strong weekday daytime population and high household incomes.
- **Building attributes**: High ceilings for play structures, ability to vent café equipment, and adequate restroom/core infrastructure.
- **Safety and compliance**: Secure entries, clear sightlines, and local code compliance for assembly + food and beverage.
