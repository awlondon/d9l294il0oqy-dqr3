# Member-Facing Training – “How to Order from Your Seat” (Video Script ~45–60s)

## Narration
**Scene 1 – Wide shot: member sitting at a workstation in Co.work.PLAY.**
“When you arrive at Co.work.PLAY, you’ll be assigned a specific seat, like A-12. Look for the seat label on your desk.”

**Scene 2 – Close-up: seat label and QR code sign.**
“On your desk, you’ll see a QR code. Scan it with your phone to sign in at your seat.”

**Scene 3 – Phone screen: Welcome / Seat Check screen.**
“The app will recognize your seat and ask you to sign in with your phone number or email. Once you’re signed in, you’ll see your session home screen with your seat and time remaining.”

**Scene 4 – Tap ‘Order food & drinks’, browse menu.**
“Tap ‘Order food & drinks’ to browse the menu. Choose your coffee, snacks, or kids’ treats, and review your order.”

**Scene 5 – Cart & payment confirmation.**
“Your order is charged to the card on your Co.work.PLAY account. Confirm, and we’ll take it from there.”

**Scene 6 – Runner bringing tray to the seat, member keeps working.**
“Our team prepares your order and delivers it straight to your seat, so you can stay focused on your work while your kids play nearby.”

**Scene 7 – Member smiles, takes drink, continues typing.**
“No lines, no juggling laptops and kids. Just sit down, settle in, and let us come to you.”
