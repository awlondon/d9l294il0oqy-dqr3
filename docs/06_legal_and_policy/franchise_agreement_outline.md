# Franchise Agreement Outline

## Purpose
High-level structure for the franchise agreement/FDD items; counsel to draft final language per jurisdiction.

## Core Sections
1. **Grant of Franchise & Territory**
   - Rights to operate Co.work.play Café within defined territory; exclusivity parameters and conditions for encroachment.
2. **Term & Renewal**
   - Initial term (e.g., 10 years) with renewal options; conditions for renewal and fees.
3. **Fees & Payments**
   - Initial franchise fee, royalties, brand fund contributions, technology fees, and any design/construction management fees.
   - Late payment interest and audit rights.
4. **Site Selection & Development**
   - Approval process for location, lease terms, design standards, and construction milestones.
5. **Operations & Standards**
   - Compliance with brand manuals, menu, safety protocols, staffing ratios, and approved vendors.
6. **Training & Support**
   - Required training hours, certification, ongoing support, and access to LMS/Sora assets.
7. **Marketing**
   - Local marketing obligations, participation in brand fund, approval for local campaigns.
8. **Technology**
   - Required POS, access control, CCTV, Wi-Fi standards; data-sharing and privacy obligations.
9. **Reporting & Audits**
   - Sales reporting cadence, KPI submission, audit rights, and consequences for under-reporting.
10. **Insurance & Indemnification**
    - Required coverage types/limits; proof of insurance; indemnity provisions.
11. **Intellectual Property**
    - Trademark usage, trade dress, proprietary materials; prohibition on reverse engineering.
12. **Transfer & Assignment**
    - Conditions for sale/transfer; franchisor right of first refusal; fees.
13. **Defaults & Termination**
    - Events of default, cure periods, immediate termination cases, post-termination obligations.
14. **Non-Competition & Confidentiality**
    - Restrictions during and post-term; protection of trade secrets and play/programming IP.
15. **Dispute Resolution & Governing Law**
    - Mediation/arbitration, venue, attorney’s fees; compliance with franchise laws.
16. **Financial Performance Representations**
    - Any Item 19 disclosures aligned with approved financial summaries.
