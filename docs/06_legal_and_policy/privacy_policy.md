# Privacy Policy (Outline)

## Purpose
Explain how Co.work.play Café collects, uses, and protects personal information for members, guests, and franchisees. Final language subject to legal review and local regulation.

## Information We Collect
- Contact details (name, email, phone), child names/ages, authorized pickups.
- Payment information processed via third-party gateways; we do not store full card numbers.
- Usage data: check-in timestamps, booking history, incident reports, and survey responses.
- Device data from Wi-Fi/POS/app (IP address, browser type) for security and analytics.
- CCTV footage for safety and loss prevention.

## How We Use Information
- Manage memberships/day passes, bookings, and payments.
- Enforce safety policies and incident response.
- Send service updates, promotional offers (opt-out available), and operational notices.
- Improve services via analytics and feedback.
- Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud.

## Sharing
- Service providers for payments, POS/LMS, marketing email, and security; bound by confidentiality agreements.
- Franchisees receive access to data for their location only; franchisor may use aggregated/anonymized data for benchmarking.
- Law enforcement or regulators when required.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Data Retention
- Account data retained while membership active and for a reasonable period after for compliance/tax purposes.
- CCTV retained for limited periods unless required for investigations.

## Security
- Encryption in transit and at rest where supported by vendors.
- Role-based access control for staff; background checks for employees with data access.
- Regular password rotation for admin accounts; MFA where available.

## Your Choices
- Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
- Request access, correction, or deletion of personal data where applicable by law.

## Children’s Information
- We collect child names/ages for safety; parental/guardian consent required.
- No marketing directed to children.

## International & State-Specific Notices
- Addendums for GDPR/CCPA or other local privacy laws as relevant to each franchise region.

## Updates
- Policy updates communicated via email/app; continued use constitutes acceptance.
