# Membership Terms of Service (Outline)

## Purpose
Provide plain-language terms for membership customers; subject to attorney review and localization per jurisdiction.

## Key Sections
1. **Membership Types & Fees**
   - Monthly family memberships include supervised play access for listed children and cowork/café access; fees auto-bill monthly.
   - Add-on hours or extra children billed per published rates.
2. **Billing & Cancellation**
   - Auto-renewal each month; member may cancel with 30 days’ notice before next billing cycle.
   - Failed payments retried; accounts paused after 7 days of nonpayment.
   - No refunds for partial months; credits may be issued for extended closures.
3. **Check-In & Identification**
   - Matching wristbands/tags required for adults and children; ID may be requested.
   - Only authorized adults on file may pick up children.
4. **Use of Facilities**
   - Members agree to follow safety rules and staff instructions; socks required in play areas.
   - Outside food/drink policies posted locally; allergy accommodations attempted but not guaranteed.
5. **Health & Conduct**
   - Do not visit when sick or symptomatic; staff may refuse entry for health/safety reasons.
   - Respectful behavior required; harassment or unsafe conduct may result in suspension or termination.
6. **Liability & Waiver**
   - Members acknowledge inherent risks of indoor play; agree to supervision policies and release of liability to the extent permitted by law.
   - Photo/video consent preferences captured at sign-up; opt-out honored.
7. **Privacy**
   - Personal data used for account management, safety, and marketing per `privacy_policy.md`; no sale of personal data.
8. **Suspension/Termination**
   - Co.work.play may suspend access for safety violations, nonpayment, or abuse of policies.
9. **Changes to Terms**
   - Notice of changes provided via email/app; continued use constitutes acceptance.
