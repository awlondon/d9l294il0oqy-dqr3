# Day Pass Terms of Service (Outline)

## Purpose
Plain-language terms for non-member guests using day passes; to be reviewed by counsel.

## Key Sections
1. **Access & Duration**
   - Day pass grants time-limited access to play and cowork zones per published hours; extensions available for a fee.
2. **Waiver & Identification**
   - All adults sign waiver; photo ID may be requested. Authorized pickup names recorded.
3. **Safety & Conduct**
   - Follow posted rules and staff directions; socks required; no outside food unless for infants/allergies per policy.
   - Caregivers remain reachable on-site; under-2 policies enforced.
4. **Health Policy**
   - Do not enter if sick/symptomatic; staff may refuse or end visit for safety.
5. **Fees & Refunds**
   - Fees due at check-in; no refunds for early departure unless facility issue forces closure.
6. **Liability & Waiver**
   - Guests acknowledge inherent risks of indoor play and release Co.work.play to the extent permitted by law.
7. **Privacy**
   - Personal data used for visit management and safety per `privacy_policy.md`.
8. **Damages**
   - Guests responsible for damage caused by misuse or willful conduct.
9. **Changes to Terms**
   - Terms may update; current version posted at check-in and online.
