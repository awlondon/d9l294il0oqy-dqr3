# Privacy Policy and Data Use (Plain-English Draft)

Draft for counsel review; applies to members, guests, and app users.

## Data We Collect
- **Account info:** Names, contact details, household members, authorized adults.
- **Payment:** Tokenized payment details via third-party processors; we do not store full card numbers.
- **Visit data:** Check-in/out timestamps, wristband/QR identifiers, incident logs.
- **Cameras & sensors:** Security cameras in public areas, occupancy/noise sensors; no cameras in restrooms or nursing rooms.
- **App/website usage:** Device/app identifiers, feature usage, and preferences.

## How We Use Data
- Manage memberships, billing, and access control.
- Support safety (matching adults/children, incident follow-up, lost-and-found).
- Improve operations and training (traffic patterns, staffing levels, AI-GM alerts).
- Send service messages, policy updates, and optional marketing communications.

## How Long We Keep Data
- Account and billing records: retained while membership is active and for required legal/accounting periods afterward.
- Camera footage: retained for a short window (e.g., 30–45 days) unless flagged for an incident or investigation.
- App/device logs: retained per performance and security needs, then aggregated/anonymized.

## What We Do Not Do
- We do **not** sell personal data.
- We do **not** use cameras or AI to “score” people or for facial recognition of children.

## Sharing & Security
- Limited sharing with processors (payments, POS, messaging, LMS) under data protection agreements.
- Law enforcement requests honored only with proper legal process.
- Security measures include role-based access, encryption in transit/at rest where supported, and staff training.

## Your Choices
- Opt-out of marketing messages at any time; service/transactional messages may continue.
- Request access, correction, or deletion of personal data (subject to legal retention requirements).
- Update photo/video consent preferences in the app or at the front desk.

## Contact
- Email privacy@co.work.play for questions or requests. Local/state privacy rights addenda will be attached where required.
