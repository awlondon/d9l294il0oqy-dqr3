# Flagship CapEx and Return Summary

## Purpose
Outline anticipated capital requirements, uses/sources, and illustrative returns for the Phoenix flagship.

## Capital Requirements (Target ~$2.2M)
- Leasehold improvements & construction: $1.25M
- FF&E (café equipment, play structure, furniture, AV): $550k
- Soft costs (design, permitting, legal): $150k
- Pre-opening (payroll, training, marketing): $120k
- Technology & security: $60k
- Contingency (10% of hard/soft): $120k
- Working capital reserve (90 days): $200k

## Sources (Illustrative)
- Equity: $1.2M
- SBA/Bank debt: $0.8M
- Landlord TI/allowance: $0.2M

## Return Illustrations (Stabilized Year)
- Revenue: ~$3.0M; EBITDA: ~$0.74M (≈28%).
- Debt service (assume 10-year, 8% on $0.8M): ≈$116k/year.
- Pre-tax cash flow after debt: ≈$0.62M.
- Simplified payback on equity (~$1.2M): ~2.0 years (excluding ramp).

## Sensitivities
- **Occupancy variance:** ±$2/SF impacts annual EBITDA by ~±$17k.
- **Labor variance:** 1% change in labor % impacts EBITDA by ~±$30k/year.
- **Membership volume:** ±25 families shifts annual revenue by ~±$96k.

## Notes
- Update assumptions if lease terms or equipment specs change.
- Excludes franchisor platform costs; see investor deck for platform funding needs.
