# Investor Pitch Narrative

## Purpose
Provide a slide-by-slide script for the investor deck to ensure consistent storytelling.

## Slide Outline & Narrative
1. **Cover** – Introduce Co.work.PLAY Café as the calm, design-forward solution for working parents; highlight café + cowork + supervised play.
2. **Problem** – Childcare gaps and noisy cafes make productive work hard; parents crave safe, clean spaces that welcome families.
3. **Solution** – Integrated hospitality model: specialty café, flexible cowork zones, and staffed play with clear safety protocols.
4. **Market** – Remote/hybrid trends, dual-income households, and employer stipend growth; TAM for family-friendly third places.
5. **Product/Experience** – Walkthrough of guest journey: check-in, café ordering, cowork productivity, safe play visibility, parties.
6. **Business Model** – Memberships + day passes + café + parties/classes + meeting rooms/retail; recurring revenue and high attach rate.
7. **Flagship Plan** – Phoenix 8,500 SF layout, pricing, revenue/cost highlights (≈$3.0M revenue, ≈28% EBITDA at scale).
8. **Traction** – Brand principles, SOPs, training content, design program, community interest/presales.
9. **Franchise Platform** – Prototype sizes, franchise fees (initial $50k, 6% royalty, 2% brand fund), support model, and unit economics (~$1.98M revenue, 22–28% EBITDA for medium format).
10. **Competitive Landscape** – Café-only, play-only, cowork-only; Co.work.play is the integrated, premium alternative.
11. **Go-to-Market** – Pre-sale memberships, employer partnerships, parent influencers, and community events; marketing stack overview.
12. **Financial Summary** – Revenue mix tables, CapEx uses/sources, payback expectations; sensitivity on occupancy and labor.
13. **Team** – Founders with hospitality, childcare, and brand backgrounds; advisors in architecture, finance, and franchise ops.
14. **Roadmap** – Flagship opening timeline, Phoenix-area cluster, and regional rollout milestones.
15. **Ask** – Capital required ($2.2M for flagship build/working capital; additional for platform) and terms (equity/debt options).
16. **Closing** – Reaffirm mission: productive parents, joyful kids, thriving communities; invite Q&A.
