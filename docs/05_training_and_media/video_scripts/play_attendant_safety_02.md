# Play Attendant Safety 02 — Floor Supervision & Cleanliness

## Narration
- “Your role is to keep play safe and calm. Rotate zones every hour to stay alert.”
- “Gently redirect rough play and guide kids to age-appropriate areas. Use simple, friendly language.”
- “When a toy gets dirty, place it in the ‘dirty toy’ bin and replace it with a clean option. Log any broken items.”
- “Watch zone capacity; if it feels crowded, call the host to meter entry.”
- “For bumps or conflicts, check on the child, notify the parent, and log the incident so the manager can follow up.”

## Visual Directions (for Sora)
- Wide shot of attendant scanning play zone, smiling at families.
- Close-up of attendant kneeling to child level, redirecting to a safer activity.
- Clip of attendant placing toy into labeled bin and wiping a surface.
- Scene showing attendant signaling to host about capacity; host nods at entry.
- Final shot of attendant entering quick note on tablet after minor incident.

## On-Screen Text
- “Rotate zones every 60–90 min”
- “Redirect gently; safety first”
- “Dirty toy ➜ bin ➜ sanitize”
- “Log incidents + update manager”
