# Flagship Five-Year Summary

Use this as a reader-friendly snapshot of the flagship financial model.

- **Revenue mix**: Memberships, day passes, café sales, and parties/events.
- **Membership assumptions**: Track member count growth, average monthly price, and utilization of play hours.
- **COGS and labor**: Model café COGS, play staffing ratios, and management oversight.
- **EBITDA and cash flow**: Summaries by year with notes on seasonality and ramp.
- **Capital needs**: Reflect the ~$2.2M CapEx plan and timeline for draws.
