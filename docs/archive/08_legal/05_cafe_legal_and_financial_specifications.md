# Co.work.PLAY Café Legal and Financial Specifications

## Franchise Agreement Summary
- **Grant of Franchise & Territory**: Provides franchisees with exclusive rights to operate within a defined geographic territory (city, ZIP code, or equivalent). Includes parameters for exclusivity, encroachment protections, and conditions for multi-unit development.
- **Term & Renewal**: Initial term of 10 years with renewal options subject to performance criteria and renewal fees.
- **Fees & Payments**: Enumerates initial franchise fees, ongoing royalties, marketing/brand fund contributions, technology fees, training fees, and transfer/closing fees. Includes payment schedules, late-payment penalties, and franchisor audit rights.
- **Site Selection & Development**: Requires franchisor approval for site selection and leases. Establishes design and construction standards, build-out timelines, and milestone approval checkpoints.
- **Operations & Standards**: Mandates adherence to brand standards, operating manuals, product/menu specifications, safety protocols, staffing ratios, and approved vendors. Franchisor may audit and enforce compliance.
- **Training & Support**: Details required initial classroom and on-site training hours, certifications, and access to ongoing support (refresher training, LMS resources, consulting).
- **Marketing**: Obligates participation in national/regional marketing programs and brand fund contributions, plus franchisor approval for local store marketing campaigns.
- **Technology**: Specifies required POS, security, access control, Wi‑Fi, and other systems; data-sharing and privacy obligations; and party responsible for equipment/software costs.
- **Reporting & Audit Rights**: Sets sales reporting cadence (monthly/quarterly) and KPIs. Grants franchisor rights to audit books/records and defines consequences for under-reporting.
- **Insurance & Indemnification**: Requires general liability, property, workers’ compensation, and umbrella coverage with proof of insurance and franchisor named as additional insured. Franchisees must indemnify franchisor and affiliates.
- **Intellectual Property & Confidentiality**: Defines authorized use of trademarks, trade dress, manuals, and proprietary systems; prohibits reverse-engineering. Includes confidentiality obligations during and after the term.
- **Transfer & Assignment**: Outlines conditions for selling or transferring franchise rights, franchisor right of first refusal, and transfer fees.
- **Defaults & Termination**: Lists defaults (non-payment, standards violations, bankruptcy, etc.), cure periods, termination rights, and post-termination duties (de-branding, non-compete, ongoing confidentiality).
- **Dispute Resolution & Governing Law**: Establishes governing law, venue, and dispute resolution process (mediation/arbitration) aligned with franchise law.
- **Franchisor Rights & Obligations**: Summarizes franchisor commitments (brand development, initial training, ongoing support) and franchisee obligations (compliance, fees, reporting). Notes exhibits/schedules such as Site Criteria, Design Guidelines, Opening Checklist, Insurance Requirements, and Manuals Acknowledgement.

## Membership & Guest Pass Terms of Service
- **Membership Types & Fees**: Defines available plans (e.g., unlimited family plan, part-time, add-on children) with included benefits (supervised play for specified children, coworking access, café discounts) and pricing. Includes auto-payment terms (monthly billing) and initial enrollment fees.
- **Billing & Cancellation**: Explains auto-renewal and cancellation policy requiring 30 days’ notice before next billing. States partial-month refunds are not provided; prorated credits may apply for extended closures. Describes late fees and service suspension after grace periods.
- **Access & Check-In**: Requires adult members and children to check in with matching wristbands/IDs. Only pre-authorized adults may pick up each child; ID checks may apply.
- **Use of Facilities**: Members and guests must follow Play Area Safety Rules (socks required, no shoes in play structures, no rough play, clean up spills, respect others’ space) and staff instructions. Outside snacks only in designated café areas; water bottles permitted. Notes age-specific rules (e.g., quiet zone for under‑2s).
- **Health & Conduct**: Visitors must be symptom-free; staff may refuse entry or end visits if illness or unsafe conduct is observed. Repeated violations can lead to suspension or termination of access.
- **Waiver & Liability Release**: Members acknowledge inherent risks of indoor play and release Co.work.play and franchisees from liability to the extent permitted by law. Guardians consent to basic first aid and emergency care. Clarifies Co.work.play is not licensed childcare and supervised play is not daycare.
- **Photo/Video Consent**: Informs visitors that photography/video may occur for safety or marketing, with opt-out instructions and usage limits.
- **Privacy & Data Use**: References the Privacy Policy for details. States member data (contact info, child info, payment details, usage history) is used for account management, safety, and communications and is not sold.
- **Suspension/Termination**: Reserves the right to suspend or terminate membership for non-payment, safety violations, facility misuse, or breach of terms, with applicable refund/credit rules.
- **Changes to Terms**: Notes that terms may be updated with notice (email or member portal) and continued use constitutes acceptance.
- **Guest/Day-Pass Terms**: Defines day-pass access (weekday/weekend, time-limited), hours of validity, and extension fees. Requires waiver signature at first visit, ID recording for adults, and authorized child pick-ups. Reaffirms safety/health policies and non-refundability for early departure (except facility issues) and assigns damage liability to guests. Reiterates privacy/data use clause and directs users to current Terms and Privacy Policy online/on-site.

## Privacy Policy Structure
- **Scope**: Applies to personal data collected from members, guests, website/app users, and franchisees. Aligns with applicable laws (e.g., CCPA, GDPR, COPPA where children’s data applies).
- **Data Collected**: Personal identifiers (name, email, phone), children’s names/ages, emergency contacts, payment info (processed by third parties), usage data (check‑ins/bookings), device/network data (Wi‑Fi/app), and CCTV footage for safety.
- **Use of Data**: Account creation/management, booking and payment processing, safety monitoring (incident tracking), notifications/promotions (with opt-outs), service improvement via analytics, and legal compliance.
- **Sharing & Disclosure**: Data shared only with service providers (payment processors, cloud/POS vendors, marketing platforms) under confidentiality, with franchisees for location needs, and with authorities as required by law. No sale of personal data.
- **Retention & Security**: Retains membership records for active duration plus tax-compliance periods; retains CCTV footage for defined intervals. Describes security measures (encryption, access controls, staff background checks).
- **Member Rights**: Explains rights to access, correct, and delete data where permitted, and to opt out of marketing, with a process for exercising rights.
- **Children’s Data**: Requires parental consent for collecting children’s information; no direct marketing to children; complies with applicable children’s data laws.
- **Updates**: Commits to policy updates for legal changes with notification (email/website). All membership/pass agreements reference this Privacy Policy.

## Licensing & Insurance Requirements (Arizona)
- **Business Licenses/Permits**: Obtain local business license/tax certificate; secure Certificate of Occupancy; obtain county health department food service permits and required employee certifications; register for Arizona Transaction Privilege Tax (TPT) license; check for sign permits, fire/safety inspections, catering licenses, and music/public-performance licenses. Confirm whether any group-care or childcare registration applies for supervised play areas.
- **Insurance Requirements**: Maintain general liability, commercial property, workers’ compensation, and umbrella/excess liability coverage; add liquor liability if serving alcohol. Franchise agreement should specify minimum coverages and require proof of insurance with franchisor as additional insured.

## Risk Mitigation and Waivers
- **Play Area Safety Rules**: Post rules covering footwear (socks required, no outdoor shoes), food/drink limits (water only in play; food in café areas), supervision expectations (adults on-site; staff not babysitters; quiet rules for under-2 area), cleanliness reporting, and behavior standards (no rough-housing, throwing toys, or climbing exteriors; respect shared spaces).
- **Waiver and Liability Release**: Include assumption of risk, agreement to comply with rules and staff instructions, medical consent for basic first aid and emergency care when guardians are unreachable, liability release and indemnification provisions, photo/video consent with opt-out, and explicit non-childcare disclaimer.
- **Operational Notes**: Require waivers at first visit and upon material updates; link waiver records to member accounts/wristbands. Train staff to enforce rules and log incidents consistently.
