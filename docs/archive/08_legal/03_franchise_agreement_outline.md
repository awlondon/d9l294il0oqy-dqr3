# Franchise Agreement Outline

- Grant of franchise, territory definition, and exclusivity parameters.
- Fees: initial, royalty, marketing fund, and optional services.
- Brand standards: design, signage, menu, uniforms, and training compliance.
- Operations: reporting cadence, inspections, and quality assurance.
- IP protection: trademarks, training materials, and proprietary systems.
- Term and renewal options; defaults, cures, and termination.
- Post-termination obligations and non-compete/confidentiality terms.
