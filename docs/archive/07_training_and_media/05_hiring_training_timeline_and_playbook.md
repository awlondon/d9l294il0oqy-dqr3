### 3.1 Relative Timeline (T-6 → T+1)
- Explain T-6, T0, etc.

### 3.2 Milestone Checklist
- Manager hired & onboarded.
- Job descriptions finalized.
- Hiring ads live.
- Shortlist created.
- Offers extended & accepted.
- Training start dates set.
- Soft openings scheduled.
- Grand opening staffed & confirmed.

### 3.3 Local Adaptation Notes
- Adjusting for shorter lead times if needed.
- Seasonal considerations (school year vs summer).

## 4. Recruiting Toolkit

### 4.1 Recommended Recruiting Channels
- Local job boards, Indeed/LinkedIn, college programs, early childhood networks, hospitality community.

### 4.2 Sample Job Descriptions
- One-page templates for each role (can be annexes).

### 4.3 Interview Guides
- Behavioral questions for Hosts (warmth, clarity).
- Scenario questions for Play Attendants (safety, behavior).
- Service and attention-to-detail questions for Café staff.
- Leadership and problem-solving questions for Manager.

### 4.4 Evaluation Rubrics
- Simple 1–5 rating for fit with brand, reliability, communication, guest empathy.

## 5. Training Curriculum Overview

### 5.1 Training Philosophy
- “Short, focused, visual” – micro-video training + written SOPs + live practice.
- Emphasis on child safety + parent experience + remote-worker needs.

### 5.2 Training Modules by Role
- Host modules (greeting, check-in flows, membership, policies).
- Play Attendant modules (safety, engagement, incident response).
- Café modules (menu, equipment, food safety, queue management).
- Manager modules (leadership, reporting, labor, guest recovery).

### 5.3 Modalities
- Sora 2–generated video clips (8–20 seconds) with voiceover.
- Longer “walkthrough” videos (e.g., 3–5 minutes per module).
- Text SOPs and checklists (PDFs and online versions).
- In-person shadowing and practice shifts.

## 6. Pre-Opening Training Plan

### 6.1 Timeline & Hours per Role
- Recommended paid training hours per role before opening (e.g., 20–30 hours for frontline; more for Manager).

### 6.2 Pre-Opening Checklist by Role
- Host: complete modules H1–H5, shadow 2 soft-open events.
- Play Attendant: modules P1–P6, safety walk-through sign-off.
- Café: modules C1–C6, menu tasting and recipe quiz.
- Manager: modules M1–M8, run at least 2 full mock shifts.

### 6.3 Soft-Opening Playbook
- How to invite friends & family.
- How to script the “test” experience.
- How to run post-event debriefs and make quick improvements.

## 7. Opening Week & First 90 Days

### 7.1 Opening Week Plan
- Extra staffing levels.
- Daily huddles (morning + close-of-day).
- Rapid feedback loop with franchisor.

### 7.2 Coaching & Observation
- Manager observation sheets: what to look for at Host, Play, Café.
- Short 1:1 coaching sessions each week for first month.

### 7.3 First 90 Days KPIs
- Membership signups and trial conversions.
- Guest satisfaction indicators (simple NPS or 1–5 rating).
- Safety: incident log trends.
- Labor vs revenue alignment.

## 8. Competency & Certification

### 8.1 Role-Based Checklists
- “Host Fully Trained” checklist.
- “Play Attendant Fully Trained” checklist.
- “Café Operator Fully Trained” checklist.
- “Manager Certified” checklist.

### 8.2 Quizzes & Scenario Tests
- Short quizzes tied to key modules (e.g., safety, food handling, policies).
- Scenario tests in person (e.g., “Deal with a lost child,” “Handle a spilled drink on the play structure stairs”).

### 8.3 Documentation & Records
- How franchisees log training completion for each staff member.
- Suggested format for audit/readiness checks from HQ.

## 9. Ongoing Training & Refresh

### 9.1 New Hire Onboarding (Post-Opening)
- Mini-version of the pre-opening curriculum.
- Integration into existing shifts & mentors.

### 9.2 Annual / Semi-Annual Refreshers
- Safety refresh; new policy updates.
- New menu or service enhancements.

### 9.3 Updates from HQ
- How HQ will deliver new videos, SOP changes, and seasonal modules.
- Expectations for franchisees to roll out updates by certain dates.
