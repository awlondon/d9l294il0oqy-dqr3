# Training Modules Overview – Co.work.PLAY Café

This document maps the core training modules for flagship and franchise locations.  
Each module is delivered via:

- Short **video clips** (often 8–20 seconds, Sora 2-generated + live footage).  
- Supporting **written SOPs** (see `docs/06_operations_and_sops/`).  
- Checklists and simple assessments where appropriate.

---

## 1. Module List

**Module 1 – Host / Front-of-House Essentials**

- Greeting members and guests.  
- Membership and reservation check-in.  
- Wristbands, stroller parking, and orientation.  
- Explaining play rules and site-specific policies.

**Module 2 – Play Attendant / Play Floor Safety**

- Opening checks for play areas.  
- Child engagement and behavior management.  
- Communicating with parents (on-site).  
- Incident reporting and basic first-response protocols.

**Module 3 – Café Operations**

- Beverage and food service basics.  
- Queue management and POS use.  
- Food safety, allergen awareness, and cleanliness.  
- Café opening and closing checklists.

**Module 4 – Manager / Store Operations**

- Scheduling and staffing.  
- Inventory and ordering.  
- Daily/weekly reporting to HQ (for franchised units).  
- Handling escalations and guest issues.

**Module 5 – Franchise Owner & Leadership Orientation**

- Overview of the brand and concept.  
- High-level financial drivers and KPIs.  
- Brand standards and compliance.  
- How to use the franchisor portal and training library.

---

## 2. Structure of Each Module

Each module includes:

1. **Intro clip** – Trainer character introduces the module, objectives, and why it matters.
2. **Micro-lessons (clips)** – Short, focused segments (e.g., “Checking in a member with a reservation”, “Handling an upset parent”, “Pre-opening café checklist”).
3. **Scenario clips** – “Good vs better” examples; typical situations staff will face.
4. **Wrap-up clip** – Summary, key reminders, and where to find written SOPs.

---

## 3. Relationship to SOP Documents

- SOPs live in `docs/06_operations_and_sops/`.  
- Video modules **illustrate** the SOPs and make them easy to learn.  
- The franchisor will host final video content in a secure, digital training platform accessible to franchisees and their staff.

This overview should be updated as we add or adjust modules for new roles or features.
