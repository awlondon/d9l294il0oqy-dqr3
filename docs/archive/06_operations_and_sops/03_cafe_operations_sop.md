# Café Operations SOP  
Co.work.PLAY Café

## 1. Role Purpose

The Café team (baristas / café operators) ensures:

- High-quality coffee and food.  
- Smooth, friendly service.  
- Cleanliness and safety in all food and beverage areas.

The café is both a **standalone experience** and a support engine for members working and watching their children.

---

## 2. Menu Overview (Template)

- **Coffee & Espresso**  
  - Drip, espresso drinks, cold brew, decaf options.

- **Non-Coffee Beverages**  
  - Teas, matcha, hot chocolate, kids’ drinks.

- **Food**  
  - Pastries, breakfast items, sandwiches, salads, light kids’ meals (as allowed by kitchen).

- **Kids & Family Items**  
  - Simple, low-mess options.  
  - Allergen information clearly posted.

Menus and SKUs will be defined per location and documented in a separate appendix.

---

## 3. Opening Checklist (Café)

Before opening to guests:

1. **Equipment check**  
   - Espresso machine: on, warmed, purged.  
   - Grinders: calibrated with dial-in.  
   - Drip brewers: ready, fresh batch times logged.  
   - Refrigeration: temp check and log (if required).

2. **Prep**  
   - Pull pastries and display items according to food safety rules.  
   - Prep simple syrups, garnishes, and toppings.

3. **Cleanliness**  
   - Counters and bar surfaces sanitized.  
   - No personal items on bar.

4. **POS & tickets**  
   - Log into POS.  
   - Test printer(s) if used for orders.

5. **Team huddle** (with Manager/Host if early)  
   - Review reservations, expected busy times, and any special events.

---

## 4. Service Flow

### 4.1 Ordering

- Most orders are taken at:
  - The **bar POS** (café), or  
  - The **Host stand** for combined check-in + café pre-pay (if configured).

- Always:
  - Confirm name for the order.  
  - Confirm any allergen info if relevant.

### 4.2 Production

- Follow standard recipes and portion sizes.  
- Call out drinks/food clearly when ready using name.  
- Priority:
  1. Time-sensitive drinks (e.g., hot espresso drinks).  
  2. Items for guests with children waiting.  
  3. Larger orders or catering trays for parties.

### 4.3 Presentation

- Keep drink surfaces clean (no spills down sides).  
- Use sleeves/lids as per safety and brand guidelines.  
- Use kid-friendly containers for children’s beverages.

---

## 5. Food Safety & Allergens

- Follow local health code at all times.  
- Never guess on allergen questions:
  - If uncertain, say: “Let me double-check that for you.”  
  - Consult recipe info or Manager.

- Keep allergen-sensitive items separate where possible.  
- Do not allow outside food in restricted areas if policy prohibits it (e.g., main play structures).

---

## 6. Cleaning During Service

- Wipe bar and café tables regularly.  
- Maintain trash at safe levels; remove before overflowing.  
- Spot mop spills immediately (or call support) and mark with a caution sign.

---

## 7. Closing Checklist (Café)

1. Turn off and clean espresso machine according to manufacturer and store procedures.  
2. Clean grinders, brewers, and surfaces.  
3. Cover or store pastries and food items per policy.  
4. Discard any products that cannot be held overnight, per food safety rules.  
5. Empty trash and recycling; wipe bins.  
6. Lock storage and back-of-house food areas if applicable.  
7. Log closing counts or waste data if required for inventory.
