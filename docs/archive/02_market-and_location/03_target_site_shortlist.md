# Target Site Shortlist – Phoenix Metro (Working Document)

> This is a **living document**.  
> All addresses, availability, and economics must be verified with current broker data.

## 1. Evaluation Framework

For each site, we rate:

- **Trade Area Fit (0–10)** – demographics, families, incomes, remote workers.  
- **Visibility & Access (0–10)** – signage, traffic, parking, stroller-friendliness.  
- **Physical Fit (0–10)** – SF, shape, ceiling heights, build-out flexibility.  
- **Economics (0–10)** – rent vs. model, TI potential, CAM, incentives.  
- **Overall Co.work.play Fit (0–10)** – holistic sense of brand and operational fit.

### Site A – West Chandler Neighborhood Center (Near Freeway & Major Intersection)

- **Description**  
  - Neighborhood/community center near a major freeway (e.g., 101 or 202) and key east–west arterial.  
  - Strong family neighborhoods, good schools, and newer housing.

- **Pros**  
  - Solid family demographics and dual-income households.  
  - Easy regional access for car-based parents.  
  - Likely ample surface parking.

- **Cons**  
  - Visibility may depend on center layout.  
  - Competing children’s concepts may already be present.

- **Preliminary Ratings**  
  - Trade Area Fit: 9/10  
  - Visibility & Access: 8/10  
  - Physical Fit: 8/10 (if 7.5–9k SF box exists)  
  - Economics: TBD  
  - Overall Fit: 8.5/10

### Site B – Arcadia / East Phoenix Corridor (Destination Flagship Potential)

- **Description**  
  - Highly visible corridor in an affluent, brand-aware neighborhood (e.g., Arcadia / Camelback-East area).  
  - Strong restaurant and lifestyle co-tenancy.

- **Pros**  
  - High-income families; strong brand halo for flagship.  
  - Good marketing value and potential press interest.  
  - Attractive for investors and franchise storytelling.

- **Cons**  
  - Higher rents; may push occupancy costs to upper range.  
  - Parking and access can be more constrained.

- **Preliminary Ratings**  
  - Trade Area Fit: 9/10  
  - Visibility & Access: 9/10  
  - Physical Fit: 7/10 (depends on finding ~8k SF)  
  - Economics: 6/10 (likely expensive)  
  - Overall Fit: 8/10 (strong flagship story if numbers work)

## 3. Next Steps

For each serious candidate:

1. Obtain **up-to-date listing info** (availability, SF, floor plans, rent, TI).  
2. Conduct **site tours** with photos and notes.  
3. Run **pro forma** using the flagship financial model:
   - Adjust rent, CAM, TI, build-out costs.
4. Decide:
   - Shortlist for LOI.  
   - Hold as backup.  
   - Eliminate.

Maintain this document as a snapshot of thinking at each stage, with dates and decision notes appended as the project moves.
