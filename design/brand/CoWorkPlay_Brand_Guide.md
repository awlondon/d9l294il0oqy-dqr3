# CoWorkPlay Brand Guide

- **Logo usage**: Primary and monoline versions; maintain clear space and avoid color alterations.
- **Color palette**: Warm neutrals with playful accent colors; ensure accessibility contrast for text.
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif for headers and body; playful script for limited accents.
- **Imagery**: Bright, candid family moments; visible safety and cleanliness cues.
- **Tone**: Warm, calm, and encouraging; speaks to parents balancing work and caregiving.

_Add logo files (e.g., `logo_primary.svg`, `logo_monoline.svg`) to this folder when ready._
